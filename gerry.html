Foundation — Priority One
Migration to Shopify:
Building the Platform.
Everything in this growth strategy depends on one foundational decision: moving Gerry from a WordPress brochure to a full Shopify e-commerce platform. Shopify is the hub that connects every marketplace, email tool, social commerce channel, and analytics platform this strategy requires.
Why Shopify Changes Everything
  • DTC revenue from day one: Customers buy directly — the sale goes to Studio Ray, not Amazon. Higher margins, owned customer relationship, owned data.
  • Email capture from every visitor: Every site visitor can be asked for their email — a permanently owned business asset that compounds with every campaign.
  • Single integration hub: Amazon, Walmart, TikTok Shop, Google Shopping, Facebook, Instagram — all connect through pre-built Shopify apps in hours, not weeks.
  • Inventory sync across all channels: One source of truth prevents overselling and enables accurate multi-channel operations.
Migration Plan — Month 1
  • Week 1: Shopify store setup — brand theme, heritage design, navigation
  • Week 2: Full catalog migration — all SKUs, imagery, descriptions transferred
  • Week 3: Payment, shipping, and fulfillment configuration — ready for first sale
  • Week 4: SEO migration — WordPress URLs redirected, rankings preserved
  • Month 2: All marketplace integrations connected through Shopify hub
Site Design Vision
  • Heritage-forward: 1946 founding story and Gerry Cunningham legacy front and center
  • Deep forest green, warm amber, clean typography — rugged outdoor energy
  • Category pages: Jackets · Pants · Shorts · Sun Protection · Kids — clean seasonal navigation
  • Performance spec callouts per product: UPF rating, water resistance, stretch — outdoor shoppers buy on specs
  • Seasonal collections: Snow Season · Summer Adventure · Spring Trail · Family Camp
What Stays the Same
  • Amazon and Walmart listings remain active — Shopify adds a new channel, not replaces
  • Costco wholesale relationship completely unaffected — wholesale stays separate
  • Brand identity, product range, and pricing architecture unchanged — infrastructure upgrade only
