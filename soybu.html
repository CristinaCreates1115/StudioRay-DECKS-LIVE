AI-powered workflows fundamentally change this equation, reducing production timelines by up to 10 times while cutting costs associated with expensive shoots and physical sampling. This is an area where I have built real, proven workflows.

Wholesale Sample Replacement

Before a physical sample is cut, AI renders can show buyers every colorway, silhouette variation, and styling option at full professional quality. This allows Studio Ray to present new Soybu collections to wholesale buyers earlier in the season, reduces the cost of producing unnecessary samples, and lets the team test buyer response before committing to production runs. The savings compound across every new season launch.

Amazon requires white background images at 2000 pixels minimum. Walmart, Target, Faire, and Instagram each have different specifications. SEO & Content Strategy
Making Soybu
Discoverable.
Soybu has a remarkable story — 26 years of performance apparel, eco-conscious materials, and a peace-inspired brand identity — but none of it is currently optimized for search discovery. Consumers are actively searching for exactly what Soybu makes. The opportunity is to meet them where they are, at the moment of intent, with content that converts interest into purchase.
AI-Powered Product SEO

Every product title and description across Shopify, Amazon, and Walmart will be rewritten using AI to target the search terms Soybu's customers are actually using. This is not keyword stuffing — it's intelligently matching product language to buyer intent, which directly improves organic ranking without any advertising spend.

  • High-intent targets: "eco-friendly yoga pants women," "recycled fiber leggings," "sustainable activewear USA," "yoga pants with pockets eco"
  • Structured data and meta tags added to all Shopify collection and product pages
  • Amazon backend keywords fully optimized for category and feature searches
Google Shopping Free Channel

Google Merchant Center integration with Shopify enables free product placement in the Google Shopping tab. This is a high-intent channel — shoppers clicking Shopping tab results are ready to buy. Setup takes hours, requires no ongoing spend, and delivers permanent organic visibility for all of Soybu's product catalog.

  • Shopify product feed connected to Google Merchant Center — immediate free placement
  • Clean product data, accurate pricing, and proper categorization maximizes organic Shopping rank
  • Every new product added to Shopify automatically appears in Google Shopping
Content & Blog Strategy

A brand with Soybu's story — eco materials, performance credentials, 26 years of product development — has extraordinary content potential that is currently untapped. An active blog targeting high-value search terms builds organic traffic over time, establishes brand authority, and supports every other channel by giving social and email content a home to link back to.

  • "Best eco-friendly yoga pants 2026" — one of the highest-volume sustainable activewear search terms
  • "Recycled fiber activewear: why it matters for your practice and the planet" — brand story SEO
  • "How to style activewear from studio to street" — lifestyle content that converts
  • "Soybu sizing guide and fit guide" — reduces returns, improves conversion rate
  • AI-assisted production: 2 posts per month, built around seasonal search trends and peak buying periods
Email Marketing — Highest ROI Channel

Email marketing consistently delivers a $42 return for every $1 spent — higher than any paid advertising channel. Currently Soybu has no visible email capture or automation in place, which means every site visitor who doesn't buy on their first visit is lost forever. This is the first thing I would fix.

  • Pop-up with offer (10% off first order) to capture emails from day one of my start
  • Welcome series: brand story, eco mission, best sellers — sets expectations and builds loyalty
  • Seasonal campaigns aligned with new collection launches and promotional moments
  • Abandoned cart recovery — automatically recaptures buyers who didn't complete checkout
